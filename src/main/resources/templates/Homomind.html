Why HOMOMinds?

HOMOMinds preserves cultural skills by giving every person a place to share knowledge, videos, stories, recipes, art, poems and traditional experiences.

It is not only for famous artists. It is for students, parents, elders, village creators, teachers and anyone with a talent to share.
